1. General Rules for Japanese Ticketing

Most tickets require a Japanese phone number and address for application. Foreign credit cards may also be rejected. We will detail the workarounds.

2. Understanding the Ticket Lottery System (抽選)

The primary method for popular events is a lottery. Joining the artist's Fan Club often increases your chances, but participation requires careful adherence to strict rules.

3. Using Official and Trusted Resale Platforms (リセール)

If you miss the initial sale, official resale sites are the safest option to avoid scams. Be cautious of unofficial third-party resellers.
